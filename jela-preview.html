About

Jon Ernest, PLA

Raised in Virginia's Shenandoah Valley, Jon Ernest is a licensed Landscape Architect dedicated to planning + design that creates, restores, and sustains the environmental, cultural, and social fabric that define communities.

Jon is the Founder + Principal of JELA Studio, a Landscape Architecture, Urban Design, and Planning firm based in Harrisonburg, VA, and serving the broader Mid-Atlantic region. Project partnerships & client relationships include both public and private stakeholders, along with consulting partnerships with multi-disciplinary teams invested in transparent and collaborative design processes.

Throughout his career, Jon has worked to blend creativity, practicality, and story-telling — designing places that maximize the project's vision while limiting disturbance and honoring the site's surrounding context and history. Professional experience spans across a range of project types and scales, including public vision and master plans, planned neighborhoods, mixed-use developments, retail and commercial districts, institutional and medical campuses, urban streetscapes, greenways, parks, plazas, and more!

Jon's work remains rooted in the belief that every project carries restorative potential — creating places that renew the environment and foster stronger, more resilient communities.
